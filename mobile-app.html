Mobile for fleets & operators

Field users stay connected to the same units and customers as the shop or fleet account — from the cab, the yard, or the roadside.

Built for the field

The RepairFront mobile app serves owner-operators and fleet-assigned drivers who need lightweight tools without a full back-office login. Update meters, capture photos, run compliance checklists, and search for breakdown help — all tied to the unit profile your shop or fleet already maintains.

What operators do in the app

  • Track odometer and hour meters on assigned units
  • Search nearby heavy-duty repair and heavy truck towing within 50 miles
  • Request Shop Connect connections to RepairFront shops (fleet accounts)
  • Upload photos and documents from the road
  • Complete DOT, BIT, and regulatory inspection checklists
  • Trigger SOS and breakdown assistance workflows

Free for one truck, fleet-ready at scale

Owner-operators can start on a free mobile tier for a single unit. Fleet operators assign units and permissions from the web platform so drivers see the right equipment and breakdown tools for their role.

Compliance features (mobile DOT/BIT forms, signed PDFs) are available as a subscription upgrade for operators who need them.
