DOT & BIT compliance

Complete annual DOT and 90-day BIT checklists on your phone, capture signatures, and share PDFs with your shop — without paper forms in the glove box.

Inspections in the field

Owner-operators and fleet-assigned drivers run structured DOT and BIT inspection forms from the RepairFront mobile app on the unit they are assigned to. Checklists follow the fields regulators expect — completed on device, not retyped later.

Sign and share

  • Capture signatures on completed inspections
  • Generate PDF records you can email or share with your repair shop
  • California fleets: BIT and Clean Truck Check workflows supported
  • Inspection history stays with the unit profile

Compliance subscription

Signed inspection workflows and PDF export are available through the Compliance subscription on the mobile app. Free owner-operator tiers include unit tracking and breakdown tools; upgrade when you need signed DOT/BIT forms.

Shop visibility

When your shop uses RepairFront, completed inspections can tie into work orders and unit records — so the office sees what the driver signed without chasing paperwork.
