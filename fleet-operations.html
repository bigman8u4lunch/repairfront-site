Fleet

Fleet operations platform

The web app where fleet managers run units, operators, vendor relationships, and telematics connections — with the same data field users see on mobile.

Your fleet command center

RepairFront fleet accounts are built for operators who manage multiple units and need visibility into breakdown tools, connected repair shops, and telematics — without a full shop back-office workflow.

What fleet managers run here

  • Unit records with make, model, engine, meters, and assignment
  • Operator users and mobile app permissions
  • Shop Connect vendor connections and connection status
  • Telematics provider links (Samsara, Geotab, CAT VisionLink) per unit
  • Critical fault visibility synced from connected telematics
  • Breakdown network usage near your fleet base

Connected to the mobile app

Assign primary units to operators so drivers see the right equipment in the mobile app for meters, breakdown search, photos, and compliance. Changes in the web app and in the field stay on the same unit record.

Works with your repair shops

When your preferred shops run RepairFront, Shop Connect links your fleet org to theirs — connected vendors surface first in breakdown search and can share unit visibility when you opt in.
