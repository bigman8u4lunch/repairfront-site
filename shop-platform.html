Repair Shop

Shop operations platform

The web app where your office and bay run day to day — customers, units, work orders, parts, invoicing, and payments in one place.

From intake to invoice

RepairFront is built for fleet and heavy-duty repair shops that need more than a generic POS. Track jobs from intake through completion with labor lines, parts tied to work orders, technician workflows, and scheduling that reflects how your shop actually operates.

What shops run here

  • Customers, fleet accounts, and unit records
  • Work orders, dispatch, and technician assignments
  • Parts sales, margins, and inventory linked to jobs
  • Invoicing, card payments, and QuickBooks sync
  • Customer portal for approvals, documents, and self-service
  • Shop settings, users, and breakdown network visibility

Connected to the rest of RepairFront

The shop platform is the hub. Field users on the mobile app, fleet Shop Connect requests, breakdown directory listings, and knowledge sharing all tie back to the same organization and customer records — so nothing is duplicated between systems.
