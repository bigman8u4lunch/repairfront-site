1. Program description

RepairFront sends transactional staff alerts (for example, customer callback follow-ups and related shop notifications) to authenticated shop users who opt in. Messages are account and operational alerts for your organization's use of the Service. This program is not a marketing, advertising, affiliate, or lead-generation messaging program.

2. Call to action / how to opt in (message flow)

End users are authenticated RepairFront staff. They opt in only after signing in to their shop account. Consent for this SMS program is collected separately from other agreements (such as the End User License Agreement) and is not bundled with unrelated terms.

  1. Sign in to RepairFront at app.repairfront.com.
  2. Open Profile and add a mobile phone number.
  3. Open Profile → Notifications. For notification types that offer Text (SMS), check an unchecked "Text (SMS)" checkbox to agree to receive recurring transactional text messages from RepairFront related to that alert type.
  4. Beside the checkbox, users are shown that message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, reply STOP to cancel, and links to these SMS Terms and the Privacy Policy. SMS remains off until the checkbox is enabled.

Example consent language shown at opt-in: "I agree to receive transactional RepairFront text alerts at the mobile number on my profile. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel. Reply HELP for help. No mobile data will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes at any time. See SMS Terms and Privacy Policy."

3. Message frequency and rates

Message frequency varies based on activity in your shop account (for example, when a customer callback follow-up is logged or a reminder is due). Message and data rates may apply. Check your mobile carrier plan for details.

4. How to opt out and get help

You can stop receiving RepairFront text alerts at any time by:

  • Replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to any RepairFront text message; or
  • Disabling Text (SMS) for the notification type under Profile → Notifications, and/or removing the mobile number from your profile.

For help, reply HELP or INFO to any RepairFront text message, or contact support@repairfront.com. Supported carriers may vary. After opting out, you may opt back in by enabling Text (SMS) again in Profile → Notifications.
