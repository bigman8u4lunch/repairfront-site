Shop Connect

Trusted fleet-to-shop connections

Fleets request connections to RepairFront shops from the breakdown map. Connected shops surface first when drivers search for heavy-duty repair or towing. Shops accept on their terms, link fleet customers, and stay the vendor of record.

Breakdown network

Shops opt in to appear when fleet customers search for nearby mobile diesel and HD repair — and heavy truck towing — during a roadside event. Visibility reflects real network usage, not paid placement.
