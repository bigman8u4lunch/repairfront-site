Smarter suggestions from your history — and the network

When equipment is linked on a work order, RepairFront suggests part lines from your shop's past jobs first, then similar make/model/engine units, then anonymized patterns from the Shop Knowledge Network. Your team confirms or rejects every suggestion — nothing is added automatically from another shop.
