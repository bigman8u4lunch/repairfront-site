Shop Connect

Trusted fleet-to-shop connections

When a unit goes down, fleets need a shop they trust — not a cold lead list. Shop Connect lets fleets request relationships with RepairFront shops and gives connected vendors priority when drivers search for help.

How it works for fleets

Fleet operators search for heavy-duty repair or towing from the mobile breakdown map. When they find a RepairFront shop, they can send a connection request — optionally with a callback number for the shop to reach them after review.

Once accepted, that shop appears as a connected vendor in future breakdown searches. Fleets can manage connections from their web settings and optionally share unit visibility with trusted shops.

How it works for shops

Connection requests arrive in shop settings under breakdown network. Shops review each request, accept or decline on their terms, and link the fleet to an existing customer record when names do not already match.

The shop stays the vendor of record. RepairFront does not resell the request as a marketing lead or broker your customer relationship to a third party.

RepairFront-only search

Fleets can toggle a “RepairFront only” filter during breakdown search to prioritize shops on the platform — including connected vendors — before expanding to the broader directory.
